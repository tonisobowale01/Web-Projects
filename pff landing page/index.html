About Us

We are the No 1 place on the web to get all your Free Fire news. We cover both the Garena Free Fire and Free Fire Max games. Our topics ranges from new features, upcoming projects in the games, tips and tricks to become a better player, access to amazing prices, competitions, and more.

Project Free Fire Prize Wheel

PFF Prize Wheel grants players access to win common, rare, legendary rewards like pets, gun skins, costumes, vehicle skins, characters, and more. Each user will be given a ticket which can be used to try out your luck in the prize wheel. Some rewards are limited. Learn more.

Moco Competitions

You will have access to participate in competitions with amazing rewards. You can win up to $10,000 in rewards. Competitions include, but not limited to, Team Deathmatch, Battle Royale, Tri Squad, Lone Wolf, etc. Learn more.
